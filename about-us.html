About Us

The United Effort Organization is a non-profit organization which supports people in need by providing them assistance and helping them become self-sufficient.

Learn More

We assist our clients through three key resources.

Affordable Housing

We provide a database of affordable housing properties in Santa Clara County to make it easier to search for below market rate apartments. It can be searched by city, waitlist status, rent, and other search terms.

Explore Affordable Housing

Essential Needs

A list of resources pertaining to food, shelter, personal care, medical care, transportation, and other needs. This list is updated on an ongoing basis.

Explore Essential Needs

General Resources

The Client Assistance Program is a crowdsourced approach to assisting people in applying for and maintaining benefits, searching for housing, and becoming self-sufficient.

Explore General Resources

About Our Clients

We serve unhoused clients as well as clients facing financial challenges. Every individual has the ability to become the best version of themselves. However, sometimes extreme financial or other challenges prevent people from achieving their goals. Our organization was founded to enable people to get back on track by using technological tools to provide effective assistance.
